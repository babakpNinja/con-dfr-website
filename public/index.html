About the Congress

A coalition of democratic forces working towards a free, federal, and secular Iran

The Congress of Democratic & Federalist Republicans (CON-DFR) is a coalition of political organizations, civil society groups, and individuals committed to establishing a democratic, federal, and secular republic in Iran. We believe in the principles of freedom, justice, equality, and the distribution of power.
