About the Congress

United for democracy, federalism, and human rights in Iran

Freedom

We stand for the fundamental freedoms of all Iranian citizens, including freedom of speech, assembly, and belief.

Justice

We advocate for a just society where the rule of law prevails and all citizens are equal before the law.

Equality

We believe in equal rights for all citizens regardless of ethnicity, gender, religion, or political belief.

Federalism

We support a federal democratic system that distributes power and respects regional autonomy and diversity.

8-Article Memorandum of Understanding

Our founding document outlines the core principles and commitments that unite all members of the Congress.

  • Commitment to democratic republican governance
  • Support for federal structure and regional autonomy
  • Protection of human rights and civil liberties
  • Secular governance and separation of religion and state
